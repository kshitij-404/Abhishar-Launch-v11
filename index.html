Abhishar is the annual magazine of AASF and also of the prestigious institute ABV-IIITM Gwalior. This magazine is a space for students to exhibit their literary talents and express their ideas and opinions.
Late Abhinav Saxena and Late Tushar Sharma are two indispensable members of AASF. The forum's and institute's annual magazine Abhishar has been named in their loving memory.
Abhishar has yet another new theme this year, A Jog Down the Sports Lane. This theme aims to celebrate the diversity and scale of this ever present element of human culture. Sports can teach us invaluable qualities and skills for our lives.
